How to Resize Pictures for JAMB UTME Registration

The JAMB registration dashboard will instantly reject any digital photograph that does not adhere to its highly unusual exact target resolution of 275 by 314 pixels. Compounding this difficulty, the final exported file size must be meticulously compressed to weigh heavier than 10 kilobytes but strictly lighter than 30 kilobytes to clear the server validation checks.

Our localized client-side application completely automates this stressful process for Nigerian students and adult citizens accessing the NIMC databases.
