Why Compress JPEGs Online?

JPEGs are the most universally accepted image format, but uncompressed photos from modern smartphones and digital cameras can easily exceed 5MB to 10MB in size. Uploading these massive files to a blog, e-commerce store, or portfolio drastically slows down your website's loading speed. Search engines penalize slow websites, and users will leave if a page takes too long to render.

Our online JPEG compressor solves this by using advanced, in-browser algorithms to intelligently reduce your file sizes by up to 80%. It strips out unnecessary metadata and gently lowers the quality matrix to a level that is practically invisible to the human eye, ensuring your photos remain crisp while saving massive amounts of storage space.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will my JPEG be compressed?

Most standard photographs will see a size reduction of 60% to 80%. The exact savings depend on the original resolution and the complexity of the colors in your image.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. We process everything directly within your web browser using HTML5 Canvas technology. Your photos remain completely private and are never uploaded, stored, or viewed by anyone else.
