How to Compress Photos for NEET & JEE Mains

The National Testing Agency (NTA) has extremely strict guidelines for document uploads on portals like NEET UG, JEE Main, CUET, and UGC NET. If your file is a single kilobyte over the limit, the system will reject it. Our NTA Document Compressor is specifically hardcoded to hit the exact safe zones for these portals so you don't have to guess.

NEET Photo Size Limits

  • Format: Strictly JPG / JPEG
  • Passport Photo Size: 10 KB to 200 KB
  • Postcard Photo Size: 10 KB to 200 KB
  • Our tool automatically targets ~80KB to guarantee crisp quality while avoiding rejection.

JEE Signature Size Limits

  • Format: Strictly JPG / JPEG
  • Signature Size: 4 KB to 30 KB
  • Thumb Impression: 10 KB to 200 KB
  • Our tool automatically targets ~20KB for signatures to ensure the upload goes through instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to upload my signature and ID here?

Yes. It is 100% private. Unlike other online compressors, we do not have a server. We use HTML5 client-side technology, which means your sensitive passport photos and scanned signatures are compressed directly inside your own web browser. They never leave your device.

Why does the NTA portal reject my photo?

The portal rejects photos for two main reasons: either the file is too large (over 200KB), or the file is too small (under 10KB). Our tool fixes this by automatically calculating a "safe zone" size in the middle of those limits, ensuring first-time success.
