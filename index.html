Frequently Asked Questions

How does client-side compression work?

Hyper Compressor utilizes the modern HTML5 Canvas API built directly into your web browser. When you drop an image, your browser analyzes and redraws the image at a smaller file size locally, without ever needing to communicate with an external server.

Which image formats are supported?

Currently, we support the most common web formats: JPG (JPEG), PNG, and WebP. Compressing a PNG usually converts it to a high-efficiency JPEG or WebP internally to achieve maximum space savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor actually private?

Yes, your privacy is 100% guaranteed. Because Hyper Compressor uses advanced client-side technology, your images never leave your computer or phone. We do not have servers that store, view, or process your files. Everything happens directly on your screen.

Will my images lose quality?

Our tool uses smart reduction algorithms to significantly shrink file sizes—compressing heavy MB files down to lightweight KB sizes—while maintaining crisp, high-quality visuals perfect for websites, apps, and sharing.

Is there a file size limit?

Because there are no server uploads involved, you are not restricted by artificial server limits. Your browser does all the heavy lifting, making the optimization process completely free and unlimited.
