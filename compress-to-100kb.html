Why Compress Images to 100KB?

The 100KB limit is one of the most universally requested file sizes on the internet. Whether you are uploading a passport photo for a visa application, submitting ID proofs to an HR onboarding portal, or uploading standard profile pictures, 100KB is the golden standard.

Because 100KB offers more "breathing room" than smaller sizes, our compressor can maintain exceptionally high visual clarity and resolution while still satisfying strict portal upload limits. Plus, your files never leave your browser.
