How to Resize Photos and Signatures for BCS Application

Applying for the Bangladesh Civil Service (BCS) through the BPSC portal requires strict adherence to image guidelines. The system will automatically reject any upload that does not meet the exact dimensions and file size limits. Our client-side tool allows you to format your documents instantly without sending your private information to external servers.

For a standard BCS applicant photo, the image must be perfectly square at 300x300 pixels with a maximum file size of 100 KB. For the applicant signature, the image must be cropped to 300x80 pixels and kept under 60 KB.
